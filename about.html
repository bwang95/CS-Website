About Me

I'm Brian Wang, an undergraduate student studying computer science at the University of Texas at Austin.

I've always known I was good with computers and have even dabbled in programming calculators before, but my formal background in the subject begins in my freshman year of high school, during which I took a basic programming course. From there, I proceeded to begin competing in UIL-style competitions (UIL stands for University Interscholastic League for those unfamiliar with the term). I discovered that I was fairly good at programming and computer science and decided to major in the subject to improve my knowledge.

I enjoy travel and have been fortunate enough to have an opportunity to live in a couple of foreign countries and visit quite a few, including Hong Kong, Malaysia, Australia, and the UK (see photos on the right), to name some of them.

I'm currently working as a lead developer for Android at Azul Mobile. We've released the Azul app; you can check us out by clicking here.

Cooking is another one of my pastimes. It may not always turn out well, but I do enjoy trying new recipes. The photo to the right documents one of the more successful attempts.

You can always find out more about my educational activities by viewing my Resume, accessible through the site navigation menu.
